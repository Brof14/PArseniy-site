I grew up in a tiny village near Biysk, surrounded by mountains. There is zero local tech scene. While most kids at school wasted their recess just killing time, I used that isolation as leverage. Outside of classes, my focus was entirely wired into the global network.

I realized a new meta early on: I don't need to write script architectures entirely from scratch to build real products. Instead, I mastered directing advanced AI development environments. My pipeline became dead simple: Idea → AI-driven architecture → MVP → Active Cashflow.

The first real battle-test was founding ByMeVPN. I deployed an automated network routing infrastructure on the VLESS protocol, optimized databases, and rebuilt the payments panel. Right after, I shipped ByMeShop — a platform handling crypto and fiat payment gateways via Python, aiogram, and PostgreSQL. Both were engineered and monetized directly from my bedroom.

To keep my execution high, I split my world between deep tech and intense physical routines. I run 3-5km, play volleyball, and hit heavy workouts multiple times a week. I'm also in my 7th year of classical training on the accordion, and I grind Anki flashcards daily to force my English to fluency.

Next milestones: transitioning to a tech lyceum, securing a silver 512GB MacBook Neo, and moving to Italy to crush a Computer Engineering curriculum in Milan. Ultimate target is Silicon Valley.
